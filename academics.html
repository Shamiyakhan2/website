Academics

SIWS College offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs that focus on academic excellence, practical learning, and skill development.

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Comprehensive understanding of commerce, finance, accounting, and business management.

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)

Strong foundation in core science subjects with opportunities for research and practical applications.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (B.Sc IT)

Focused on programming, databases, networking, and modern IT solutions for the digital era.

Postgraduate Programs

Master of Commerce (M.Com)

Advanced learning in commerce, finance, and business strategies.

Master of Science (M.Sc)

Specialized knowledge in science disciplines with research opportunities.

Master of Science in Information Technology (M.Sc IT)

In-depth focus on advanced IT concepts, data science, and software development.
